The user is running a TurtleBot 4 (Raspberry Pi 4 + iRobot Create 3 base) for the CS7980
"guide robot" project and needed it onto the university enterprise WiFi (NUwave / 802.1X)
because connecting to that network was stated as "an absolute requirement"
