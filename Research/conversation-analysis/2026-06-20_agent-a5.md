## Motive

The user (operating robot 468, a TurtleBot 4 with an OAK-D-LITE camera on a Raspberry Pi 4)
asked the agent to research Linux/Raspberry Pi 4 USB-level techniques to both PREVENT and
RECOVER an OAK-D-LITE that disconnects or wedges, without physically replugging the device.
The request was a focused research task tied directly to a known hardware gotcha (the OAK
"camera_node alive but 0 Hz / X_LINK stall" failure) and asked for a copy-pasteable markdown
report with exact commands, kernel params, a watchdog/recovery script skeleton, and cited URLs
(file: 2026-06-20_agent-a5.jsonl, date: 2026-06-20, turn: 1).
