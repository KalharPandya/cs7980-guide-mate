## Motive

documentation file (`docs/network/ros2-over-nuwave.md`) in a ROS2 TurtleBot 4 "guide robot"
project, judge its importance and accuracy, and flag any factual drift between the doc's
claims and the actual code/config inventory on disk (file: 2026-06-20_agent-af.jsonl,
date: 2026-06-20, turn: 1).
