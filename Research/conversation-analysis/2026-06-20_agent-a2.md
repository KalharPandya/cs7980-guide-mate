## Motive

The session is a synthesis task. The user supplied a large body of web/driver research into OAK-D-LITE camera and depth failures on a Raspberry Pi 4 (robot turtlebot468, ROS 2 Humble, depthai_ros_driver 2.29.0) and asked Claude to consolidate it into a single authoritative report, reconciled against the live repo and robot state (file: 2026-06-20_agent-a2.jsonl, date: 2026-06-20, turn: 1). The goal was a debugging/diagnosis artifact: identify the real root cause of random USB disconnects, build a recovery playbook, and lay out concrete on-robot experiments and a watchdog design.

- Prevention table (usbfs_memory_mb, autosuspend, DEPTHAI_WATCHDOG, queue sizes, Y-adapter external power) (turn: 9).
- Seven concrete on-robot experiments to discriminate the hypotheses (turn: 9).
- An external hz-probe watchdog script plus systemd unit and sudoers entry (turn: 9).
